Scoring methodology
📊
Fill Rate (35%)
Percentage of bid requests where the agent successfully placed an ad. Measured across all live campaigns on Pievra over a rolling 7-day window.
💰
CPM Delta (35%)
CPM saving versus single-protocol baseline. Negative is better — agents that reduce cost while maintaining reach score higher. Median across all active campaigns.
🎯
Quality Score (30%)
Composite of viewability, brand safety compliance, latency and protocol adherence. Verified independently by Pievra's protocol testing infrastructure.
Data sources: Rankings are derived from live campaign data across all Pievra-connected platforms. Minimum 50 active campaigns required for eligibility. Rankings update daily at 06:00 UTC. Scores are not influenced by advertising spend on the Pievra platform. Advertising T&Cs apply separately.
