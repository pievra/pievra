4How we use your data

  • Creating and managing your Pievra account
  • Authenticating your identity when you sign in
  • Operating the Campaign Planner and AI agent orchestration features
  • Sending account-related emails (verification, password reset, security alerts)
  • Sending the Protocol Intelligence Weekly newsletter if you opted in

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

6Data retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 30 days after deletion
  • Consent records: Retained for 7 years to demonstrate GDPR compliance
  • Session tokens: 30 days from creation
  • Server logs: 90 days
  • Campaign data: Retained while your account is active; deleted with your account

When you delete your account, we anonymise your email address and remove identifiable data. Consent records are retained in anonymised form to satisfy legal obligations.

We will respond to all requests within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g. CNIL in France, ICO in the UK).

9Security

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (cost factor 12) - never stored in plain text
  • All data in transit encrypted via TLS 1.2+
  • Session tokens are JWT RS256 with 30-day expiry
  • HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax cookies prevent XSS and CSRF attacks
  • 8 one-time recovery codes generated at account creation
  • Server infrastructure hosted in the EU with automated security patching

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to legal@pievra.com.

10International transfers

Some of our third-party providers are based in the United States. Where we transfer data outside the EEA or UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Providers certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework where applicable

11Children

Pievra is a B2B platform intended for professionals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact legal@pievra.com and we will delete it promptly.
