1Definitions

"Advertiser" means the company, individual or entity submitting an application for a featured placement.

"Agent" means the AI software agent or tool being advertised on the Pievra marketplace.

"Campaign" means an active featured placement running on the Pievra marketplace during a defined period.

"CPI" means Cost Per Impression - the price charged for each verified buyer impression delivered.

"Daily Budget" means the maximum amount the Advertiser authorises Pievra to charge per calendar day.

"Impression" means a single verified display of the Advertiser's agent listing to a qualified buyer on the Pievra platform.

"Pievra" means the platform operated at pievra.com.

"Placement" means the specific position on the Pievra marketplace where the Agent is displayed.

Pievra reserves the right to reject any application at its sole discretion without obligation to provide reasons.

Approved applications will receive a confirmation email within 1 business day with next steps and payment instructions.

3Pricing, billing and invoicing

3.1 Pricing model

Advertising on Pievra is priced on a Cost Per Impression (CPI) basis with a daily budget cap set by the Advertiser. Current CPI rates are:

  • Standard Listing: $0.07 per impression (minimum $25/day)
  • Hero Placement: $0.18 per impression (minimum $50/day)
  • Sponsored Newsletter: $0.35 per impression (minimum $100/send)
  • Custom Campaign: contact us for details

Pievra reserves the right to update CPI rates with 14 days' written notice to active Advertisers.

3.2 Billing

Billing occurs in arrears on a monthly basis for the actual impressions delivered during the billing period, subject to the Advertiser's daily budget cap. Payment is processed via Stripe using the payment method on file.

3.3 Invoicing

Advertisers may request a formal VAT invoice for each billing period. To request an invoice:

  • Email legal@pievra.com with your company name, registered address, VAT/tax number and billing period
  • Invoices are issued in USD and EUR upon request
  • Invoices are issued within 5 business days of the billing period close
  • Invoices are issued automatically for active advertising accounts

3.4 Late payment

Campaigns will be paused if payment fails or is not received within 7 days of the due date. Pievra reserves the right to charge interest on overdue amounts at 2% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower.

4Campaign budget and delivery

The Advertiser sets a daily budget cap. Pievra will not charge more than the daily cap in any calendar day. Unused daily budget does not roll over to subsequent days.

Delivery of impressions is subject to available inventory and buyer activity on the platform. Pievra does not guarantee delivery of the full daily budget on any given day.

Advertisers may pause, resume or cancel campaigns at any time via the dashboard or by contacting legal@pievra.com. Paused campaigns are not charged for the paused period.

Pievra uses impression-level tracking. An impression is counted when the Agent listing is rendered and visible to a verified buyer for a minimum of 1 second.

5Advertiser content standards

All creative content submitted by the Advertiser - including agent descriptions, logos, performance claims and protocol badges - must be:

  • Accurate - performance metrics, deployment numbers and capability claims must be verifiable
  • Non-misleading - no exaggerated claims, simulated results or fabricated buyer testimonials
  • Protocol-compliant - claimed protocol support (AdCP, MCP, UCP, ARTF, A2A) must be technically verified by Pievra prior to display
  • Legal - content must comply with all applicable advertising laws including CAP Code (UK), EU Digital Services Act and US FTC guidelines
  • Brand-safe - no content that is offensive, discriminatory, politically sensitive or harmful

Pievra reserves the right to require amendments to or remove any content that does not meet these standards without issuing a refund.

6Pievra verification requirements

Before a campaign goes live, Pievra conducts a technical and compliance review including:

  • Protocol endpoint testing for each claimed protocol
  • Security review of agent API and authentication
  • Review of performance claims for substantiation
  • GDPR and data handling assessment for agents that process personal data

The Advertiser must cooperate with this review and provide access to technical documentation, API endpoints and test environments as requested. Failure to complete verification within 10 business days of application approval may result in cancellation without refund of any setup fees.

Verification does not constitute endorsement by Pievra of the Agent's performance or suitability for any particular use case.

7Performance and reporting

Advertisers receive access to a real-time analytics dashboard showing:

  • Impressions delivered (daily and cumulative)
  • Profile visits (clicks to agent detail page)
  • Deploy enquiries (buyer intent signals)
  • Estimated reach by protocol and buyer segment

All metrics are updated within 24 hours. Pievra's impression count is the definitive figure for billing purposes. Discrepancies of more than 10% between Pievra's count and any third-party measurement tool must be reported within 30 days of the billing period close to be eligible for credit.

8Cancellation and refunds

  • Standard and Hero placements may be cancelled at any time. The Advertiser is charged only for impressions delivered up to the cancellation date.
  • Sponsored Newsletter placements may be cancelled up to 5 business days before the scheduled send date for a full refund. Cancellations within 5 business days of the send date are non-refundable.
  • Custom campaigns are subject to the cancellation terms agreed at the time of booking.
  • Pievra may cancel a campaign for breach of these terms. In such cases, charges for impressions already delivered remain payable.

Refund requests must be submitted to legal@pievra.com within 30 days of the charge. Refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method.
