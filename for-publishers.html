Revenue Model

Transparent, predictable fees

Pievra charges a single liquidity fee on transactions that flow through the platform. There are no listing fees, no minimum commitments and no hidden charges.

The fee is deducted from the buyer's payment before settlement. As a publisher, the price you set is the price you receive, minus the liquidity fee.

This model aligns Pievra's incentives with yours: we only earn when your inventory transacts.
